What you'll do: You'll complete 3 online tests (each takes 15-20 min). I'll send you a personalised link for each:
1. MOS Test (9 clips) — Listen to each clip fully, then rate it on 5 musical quality criteria (e.g. Structural Coherence, Musical Flow) using a 1–5 scale (Bad → Excellent).

3. Turing Test (10 clips) — Listen to each clip and decide: Human-composed or AI-generated? Trust your instincts!
